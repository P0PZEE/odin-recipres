Tomato Pasta

Tomato Pasta , a classic dish that was a lot of nutrition and will fill you up for some time, so What do we need for this Simple Dish

Ingredients:

  • 2 Cups Penne Pasta
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 small yellow onion, copped (about 1/2 Cup)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 Cups canned crushed tomatoes
  • Salt and Chili Flakes to Taste

Instructions:

  1. First: Melt the Better and Oliver Oil in a Medium skillet, Add the chopped onion and cook over medium heat until it is soft and translucent, about 5 to 6 minutes
  2. Second: Add the minced garlic and cook, stirring, for an additional 15 seconds. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and bring to a simmer. Season with chili flakes and salt to taste. Let it simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Third: Meanwhile, bring a Medium pot of salted water to a boil. cook the penne until al dente, about 10 to 11 minutes, or according to the package Instructions. Drain the pasta, Saving 1/4 cup of Pasta Water.
  4. Fourth: Add the pasta along with the reserved pasta water to the tomato sauce. Stir well to coat and cook together for an additional minute to blend the flavors. Adjust the seasoning to taste and serve immediately.
