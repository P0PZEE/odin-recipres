Spiced Chickpeas with Yogurt

Spiced Chickpeas with Yogurt This is a Dish i like to eat as my breakfest as im not much for sweets and specially in the morning, so What do you need for this Quick Dish

Ingredients:

  • 100 Grams of Yogurt
  • 1 can of cooked Chickpeas
  • Oilve Oil
  • Salt, powderd Cumin, Chili Flakes and Paprika
  • 1 Tablespoon Tomato Paste
  • 1 Glove of Garlic

Instructions:

  1. First: Heated up the Oilve Oil on Medium in a pan, than drain the Chickpeas and wash them a few times before add adding them to the pan
  2. Second: After 2 minutes, Add the tomato paste and stir until well compared then add Salt Cumin Paprika and Chili Flakes to to Taste, then drop the heat to low for 5 minutes
  3. Third: Meanwhile, in a Bowl add the Yogurt, minced garlic and some salt
  4. Fourth: After the Chickpeas are done add them to the bowl and mix

Enjoy
